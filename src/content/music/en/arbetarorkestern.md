Arbetarorkestern — "the workers' orchestra" — is a collective world-music
ensemble based in Stockholm. The name carries the weight of the Swedish prog
and working-class ballad tradition, but the music reaches far beyond any one
country: West African grooves, southern African call-and-response, Ugandan
songs of exile, roots reggae, Latin American nueva trova, and Swedish poets
like Cornelis Vreeswijk and Mikael Wiehe all share the stage.

Highlights so far: a memorial concert for the Namibian musician Tauna
Niingungo at the Museum of Ethnography (September 2025), and Tidafestivalen
in June 2026. The repertoire spans thirteen languages.
