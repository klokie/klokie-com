## 1. **Find Hidden Deals Others Miss**

> "Act as a travel expert. Search for the cheapest round-trip flights from [City] to [Destination] in [Month/Date Range], using lesser-known airports and flexible dates."

## 2. **Bypass Location-Based Pricing**

> "How can I use a VPN, browser settings, and local currencies to lower airfare prices while booking from [my country]? Give a step-by-step strategy."

## 3. **Stack the Best Tools**

> "List the most underrated tools, extensions, or websites that track flight deals, mistake fares, or flash sales — explain what each is best used for."

## 4. **Reverse-Engineer the Best Booking Days**

> "Based on flight pricing trends, what are the best days/times to book international flights from [Country] to [Continent] at the lowest possible cost?"

## 5. **Trigger Hidden Discounts**

> "Give me scripts or tricks to unlock student, group, or unpublished flight discounts even if I’m not eligible. Think customer service, language, and booking tactics."

## 6. **Design a Complete Travel Workflow**

> "Build a weekly system using ChatGPT, Google Flights, and alerts to monitor and auto-track the cheapest flights to my 5 dream destinations for 2024."

## 7. **Predict Price Drops Like a Pro**

> "Act as a predictive analyst. Based on historical trends and current demand, what are the chances of flight prices from [City] to [City] dropping in the next 30 days?"
