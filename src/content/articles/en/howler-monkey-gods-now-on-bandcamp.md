At long last, all released tracks by Howler Monkey Gods are now available
for purchase — or to stream for free — on
[Bandcamp](https://howlermonkeygods.bandcamp.com/)!

Diesel-powered melodic metal from Stockholm. Howler Monkey Gods were a
four-piece drawing from grunge, black metal, and prog — somewhere between
Alice in Chains, Mastodon, and Queens of the Stone Age. Four self-produced
EPs, 2009–2014.
