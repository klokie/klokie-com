Use the following prompts with Chat GPT and GPT5 to find the best flight deals!

**1. Find Hidden Flight Deals**You’re a travel hacker. Find me 5 international flight deals from [Your City] under $300 in the next 60 days. Prioritize major cities, minimal layovers, and hidden fare tactics.**2. Trigger Price Drops**Build a plan to track price changes for flights to [Destination] using free tools and alerts. Include steps for catching flash sales and off-peak rates.**3. Bypass Expensive Dates**Act like a flight analyst. Show me the cheapest round-trip date combos from [City] to [City] in [Month], focusing on weekdays and flexible returns.**4. Beat the System**You’re a former airline insider. Share 3 overlooked booking tricks that can instantly lower airfare.**5. Optimize Airport Choices**

List cheaper alternate airports near [City], and compare average fares. Include travel time and cost to each one.
