### Pioneering E-Commerce for Tiffany & Co.: A Digital Milestone in 1999

In 1999, I helped architect and build Tiffany & Co.'s first e-commerce website, designed by OvenDigital. This groundbreaking site, featuring both Flash and HTML elements, brought the world-renowned jeweler's luxury shopping experience online for the first time. The innovative design and user-friendly functionality quickly translated into commercial success, generating over $1 million in its first few months. This project set a new standard for luxury e-commerce and demonstrated the potential of digital platforms in the retail industry.
