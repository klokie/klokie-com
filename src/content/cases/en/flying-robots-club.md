The Flying Robots Club, founded by Heidi Harman (who also started Geek Girl
Meetup), ran robot-building workshops aimed at fostering creative confidence in
teenage girls. Sessions took place in both Stockholm and Brazil, in
collaboration with the Swedish Embassy and KTH (the Royal Institute of
Technology). Hands-on building, international cooperation, and young girls
getting their hands on technology and innovation.
