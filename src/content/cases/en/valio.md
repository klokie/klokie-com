## History

Valio, established in 1905, is Finland's leading dairy producer, known for its wide range of products and innovation in functional foods ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Valio)). As the lead developer for Valio's website, I played a pivotal role in transforming and maintaining its online presence. Here's an inside look at the key projects and improvements implemented during my tenure.

### Custom Campaigns

Creating dynamic, engaging campaigns was essential for enhancing user interaction. Utilizing Drupal 7's flexibility, I developed custom modules that allowed for personalized content delivery. These included product recommendations and seasonal promotions tailored to user behavior, driving higher engagement and conversion rates.

### Performance Enhancements

Ensuring the website's performance was a priority given its extensive product catalog and high traffic:

-  **Caching Optimization** : Implemented Varnish and Memcached to serve cached content efficiently, reducing load times.
-  **Database Tuning** : Optimized MySQL queries and indexing for faster data retrieval.
-  **Front-End Optimization** : Minimized and aggregated CSS and JavaScript files, and implemented lazy loading for images, enhancing page load speed.

### Seamless Integrations

To provide a seamless user experience, I integrated various third-party services:

-  **CRM Systems** : Connected our CRM for a unified view of customer interactions.
-  **Coupon & PR Gateways** : Enabled secure and efficient online transactions.
-  **Social Media** : Enhanced engagement with social sharing capabilities and social login features.

### Security Measures

Maintaining robust security was crucial:

-  **Regular Updates** : Ensured all modules and core updates were promptly applied.
-  **Security Audits** : Conducted regular audits to identify and address vulnerabilities.
-  **Custom Security Solutions** : Developed additional security layers like two-factor authentication.

### Conclusion

Working with Valio was a rewarding experience, combining technical challenges with significant business impact. The enhancements we made ensured Valio's website was robust, secure, and highly engaging for users, reflecting the company's innovation and leadership in the Nordic dairy industry.
