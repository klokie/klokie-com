A browser-based tone generator for the solfeggio frequencies — the set of tones
rooted in a medieval hymn to John the Baptist and rediscovered in the 1970s.
Pick a frequency (174 Hz through 963 Hz), shift it by octave, and change the
waveform; every tone is synthesized live through the Web Audio API, no audio
samples anywhere. A small, self-contained tool built for its own sake.
