Co-founder & CTO of Surface to Air (New York/Paris), a design studio
providing website and design services for major and independent media
companies in publishing, music, film, and fashion across Europe, the US, and
Asia. Website clients included Source Records, National Alliance of Breast
Cancer Organizations, Spoon Magazine, MoMA, Independent Pictures, and Virgin
UK. The studio worked with and exhibited artists including Donald Baechler,
Banksy, Julian Schnabel, Futura 2000, and Alejandro Jodorowsky, and musicians
including Amon Tobin, Feist, Gonzales, and Phoenix.
