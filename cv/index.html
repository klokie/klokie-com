1. ##### KENDRA INITIATIVE

###### Drupal Web Architect

2009-09 - present, Stockholm, Sweden

Software architect (Drupal, Javascript, scalability/performance), semantic technology consultant, and liaison to the European Union for the SARACEN project, funded under the EU’s FP7 framework for technological innovation.

Researching, designing and building scalable architectures using open source components (Drupal, Apache, Apache Solr, Apache Mahout, Open Link Virtuoso, MySQL, Memcached), web services APIs (Facebook, Twitter, Google, dbPedia, OAuth, OpenID, OpenSocial), and languages (PHP, Javascript/JQuery/JSON, Python, SQL, SPARQL, Atom, RDF/XML, HTML5).

Currently working on proposals for user and media profile integration for federated social networking and user-controlled privacy preferences.
2. ##### YEPLER/FILERIDE

###### Co-founder, Director of Community & Web Services

2007-06 - present, Stockholm, Sweden

As co-founder, co-architect and principal front-end engineer of both a unique international social network and a patent-pending semantic content management system, Klokie created the client interface and real-time web services API and continues to build an international community around Stockholm-based startup Yepler.

Designed and built three major iterations of client-side software and web services (PHP, Javascript/JQuery/JSON, CSS, XHTML). Customized, integrated, optimized and tested scalable back-end architecture based on Tornado (Python). Integrated third party web service APIs (Facebook, Twitter, Freebase, Google, dbPedia, Wikipedia) using Javascript and JQuery.

Consulted on long-term strategy, branding, coaching, business model development, and financial goals. Helped develop a winning pitch for seed funding round.
More information and software available at** YEPLER.COM.
3. ##### SURFACE TO AIR, SARL & LLC

###### Chief Technical Officer

2000-04 - 2008-10, Paris / New York / Barcelona / Tokyo

As co-founder of the multidisciplinary international creative studio Surface to Air, developed online strategies for leading brands in major US and European music, design and fashion markets. Delivered an all-purpose e-commercle platform with first year sales of over US$1.5M for electronic music gurus BreatBeatScience.com. Created one of the first wiki publishing systems for the National Alliance of Breast Cancer Organizations in 2001. Launched two Paris-based boutiques and clothing labels. More work history available online or by request.**
4. ##### OVEN DIGITAL, Inc.

###### Senior Software Developer

1998-10 - 2000-04, New York, NY

Best Art Museum Site in 2001), and crafted a pioneering rich-media experience for Digital Club Network (winner of OneShow “Gold Pencil” award, and now run by Live.com).
5. ##### HARMONIX MUSIC SYSTEMS, INC.

###### Network Administrator

1997-06 - 1998-08, Cambridge, MA

One of the first employees at the gaming start-up which would later become world famous for creating the classic “Guitar Hero”, Klokie administered an ISDN-based network of Windows NT and Exchange Servers, Microsoft Visual SourceSafe, and high-performance Macs and PCs in addition to creating and managing public and private Web and FTP sites. Utilizing a deep knowledge of music theory, Klokie also performed encoding and harmonic analysis of digital audio files using proprietary Perl routines.
