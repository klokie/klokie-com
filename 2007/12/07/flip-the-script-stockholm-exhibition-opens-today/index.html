'Flip the Script' Stockholm exhibition opens today

December 07, 2007

Thanks to the support of the good folks over at A World Beneath and the hard work of Sandra Myhrberg and myself, last night’s opening party for Surface to Air’s book “Flip the Script“ was a great success. The exhibition at the Urban Outfitters Stockholm store located in the magnificent former Roda Kvarn Cinema (address & open hours here) will be open until February 2008. We’re also selling the books (including 50 large format, limited edition posters) for for a special holiday price of 5000 SEK including taxes and shipping.

Press mentions at Bon Magazine, Rodeo Magazine, Feber, conspicere, klangblogg, and Stureplan.
