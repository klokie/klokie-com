OStatus sandbox

Fresh from a great week at FSW 2011 in Berlin, I’ve just set up the OStatus suite of plugins for WordPress. Just experimenting with Webfinger, Activity Streams, and PuSH at the moment to see how well this works, but my grand plan is to migrate this (out of date) space to Drupal 7 and combine it with my (also well aged) online portfolio when I have a few moments over the summer. Ping me in the comments if you want to test, or find me via Webfinger.
