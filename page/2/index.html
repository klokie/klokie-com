Surface to Air 'Flip the Script' release party Stockholm this Thursday!

Välkomna på vernissage den 6 december kl 18-20.30!
Designkollektivet Surface to Air lanserar konstboken FLIP THE SCRIPT, en samling omtolkade klassiska filmaffischer av Shary Boyle, José Parlá och Lansing Dreiden m.fl. I samband med releasen presenteras de unika verken i galleriet på Urban Outfitters. Boken kommer också att finnas till försäljning under kvällen.

Welcome to the Stockholm vernissage 2007-12-06 18:00-20:30!
The design collective Surface to Air is launching the art book FLIP THE SCRIPT, a collection of redesigned film posters as envisioned by Shary Boyle, José Parlá, and Lansing Dreiden among others. The release party for this limited-edition work is in coordination with Urban Outfitters Stockholm with after party around the corner at Lila Baren, Riche. The book will also be available for sale at a special holiday price at the event.
