Surface to Air 'Flip the Script' limited edition book now available


“Flip the Script”, the exquisitely boxed collection of 49 movie posters reinterpreted by world-renowned artists is finally available to the public. With otherwise unavailable work by Wes Lang, Faile, Phil Knott, Nagi Noda, José Parlá, Lansing Dreiden, RoStarr, and Kenzo Minami among numerous noteworthy others, the book is available from the Surface to Air website in a limited edition run of 500. Extra-large at 70 cm tall by 50 cm wide (28 x 20 inches).

Dante Aaron Wolfgang Grossfeld

170

My son, Dante Aaron Wolfgang Grossfeld, was born today at 12:45 Swedish time.
Weight: 3840 grams (8 pounds, 7 ounces)
Height: 52 centimeters (20.5 inches)
Location:Södra BB: Stockholm, Sweden

After over 10 hours of near-exorcism, Sandra (with the help of 2 doctors and 5 midwives) delivered unto the world a beautiful baby boy. The staff at Södersjukhuset that has been looking after us - about 20 “barnmorskar” (midwives) at least - has been more caring, knowledgeable, and professional than I could possibly have hoped. The whole experience, although it was very stressful during the extra 5 days we had to stay in the hospital due to a slight fever and endless crying over the weekend, has been an overwhelmingly positive one.
